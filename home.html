I am a Robot Vision researcher with expertise in 3D Perception, Sensor Fusion, and Computer Vision. Currently, I'm a Senior Applied Scientist working on the Astro robot at Amazon Lab126. My work focuses primarily on depth sensors and obstacle mapping algorithms.

Previously, I was a Computer Vision engineer in the Home Applied Intelligence team at Apple.

Prior to Apple, I built the Depth Perception stack for the Lighthouse AI camera which functions as a search engine for your home. Various media outlets (Gizmodo, Security Baron and many more) have rated Lighthouse as the best camera in the AI category. Check it out here.

I graduated with a Ph.D. in Exploration Systems Design at Arizona State University. This website summarizes the various projects I worked on during my grad school. To know about my more recent work, please contact me on LinkedIn.

During my Ph.D., I was affiliated to the ASTRIL lab headed by Prof. Srikanth Saripalli at the School of Earth and Space Exploration, Arizona State University. Prior to that, I was part of the Robotics Research Center, IIIT Hyderabad, headed by Prof. Madhava Krishna.
